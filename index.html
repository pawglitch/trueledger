Coinbase Prediction Markets

Where to find your transactions:
1. Open coinbase.com (or the app) and go to Predictions
2. Open your Activity / transaction history and scroll to the bottom so every entry is loaded
3. Select the whole list — Ctrl+A (Cmd+A on Mac) — then copy with Ctrl+C
4. Click in the box below and paste with Ctrl+V — a preview appears so you can check it looks right
5. Hit Analyze

Polymarket

Polymarket trades are on-chain, so no export is needed — just your wallet address.
Where to find your address:
1. On polymarket.com, click your profile picture → your profile
2. Copy the address that starts with 0x (or just copy your profile page URL)
3. Paste it below — your history is fetched read-only from Polymarket's public API
🔒 Read-only public data — no keys, no signatures, nothing to connect.
